Types of Pergolas We Install Across Dublin

Free Standing Pergolas

A great choice for creating a standalone garden retreat, barbecue area, or stylish focal point. These pergolas are perfect for patios, lawns, and anywhere you want to create a defined outdoor space.

Wall Mounted Pergolas

Ideal for smaller gardens or patios, wall-mounted pergolas are attached directly to your home. They provide seamless shade and shelter while saving space and enhancing the aesthetic of your garden.

Roofed Pergolas

Get protection from rain or strong sun with a fully roofed pergola. We offer options like polycarbonate, louvered, or tiled roofs — perfect for Irish gardens that need shelter all year round.

Pergola Costs in Dublin – What to Expect

Pergola prices in Dublin vary depending on the size, materials, and type of roof. Here's a general breakdown:

  • Timber Pergolas: from €1,600 – €3,500
  • Aluminium Pergolas: from €2,500 – €6,000+
  • Roofed Pergolas (louvered or polycarbonate): from €3,000 – €7,500+
