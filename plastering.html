Why choose our plastering services?

The exterior finish of your garden walls and structures plays a crucial role in both their appearance and durability. At Central Landscaping, our team offers professional plastering, rendering, and dashing services to enhance the look and longevity of your outdoor masonry surfaces.

Whether you're building new garden walls, pillars, or other structures, or looking to refresh existing ones, our skilled plasterers can provide high-quality finishes that protect your masonry while complementing your overall landscape design. We specialize in creating finishes that seamlessly integrate with your home's exterior or create striking contrasts, depending on your preferences.

Our plastering services include traditional sand and cement rendering, modern acrylic renders, and traditional dashing (roughcast) finishes. Each has distinct characteristics and benefits, and our experienced team can advise on the most suitable option for your specific project.

What sets our plastering services apart is our attention to detail and preparation. We understand that the key to a long-lasting, attractive render or dash finish is thorough preparation of the underlying surface. Our team ensures that all masonry is properly prepared, any repairs are completed, and appropriate priming is done before applying the final finish.

We take particular care with corners, edges, and junctions, ensuring clean, crisp lines that enhance the appearance of your walls and structures. Our plasterers are skilled in creating various textures and finishes, from smooth contemporary renders to textured traditional finishes, allowing you to achieve exactly the look you desire.

The benefits of professional rendering or dashing extend beyond aesthetics. These finishes provide essential protection against the Irish weather, preventing water penetration and subsequent damage to masonry. They can also improve the thermal performance of walls and extend their lifespan significantly.

Our team can match new render or dash to existing finishes on your property, ensuring a cohesive look when extending or adding new garden features. We can also incorporate colored renders or specialized finishes to create distinctive, eye-catching features in your garden landscape.

Based in Dublin, we serve clients throughout the greater Dublin area. Contact us today to schedule a consultation and discover how our plastering, rendering, and dashing services can enhance the appearance and durability of your garden walls and structures.
